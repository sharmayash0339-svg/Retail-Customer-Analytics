# Statistical Deep Dive: Customer Segmentation & RFM Analysis
Optimizing Marketing ROI through Data-Driven Insights

# Project Overview
This project performs a comprehensive statistical analysis of customer purchasing behavior to identify high-value segments and improve retention.
By leveraging **SQL, Python, and Power BI**, I transformed raw transactional data into actionable business strategies using the **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)** framework.

# Business Problem
The company is currently using a "one-size-fits-all" marketing approach, resulting in:
 **High Churn Rates:** Difficulty in identifying customers about to leave.
 **Inefficient Spending:** Marketing budget wasted on low-value customers.
 **Missed Revenue:** No personalization for VIP/Loyal customers.

**Objective:** Segment the customer base into distinct groups to enable targeted marketing campaigns and increase Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

# Tech Stack
 **Excel:** Initial data exploration and quick profiling.
 **SQL (PostgreSQL/BigQuery):** Data aggregation and feature engineering.
 **Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn, Seaborn):** Statistical analysis, K-Means Clustering, and RFM scoring.
 **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard for executive reporting.

# Dataset Description
The analysis utilizes three primary datasets (simulated retail data):
1. customers_dataset_10000`: Unique customer identifiers and location data.
2. orders_dataset_12000`: Order history with timestamps and status.
3. order_items_dataset_30000`: Item-level pricing and volume details.

# Analysis Workflow
# 1. Data Cleaning & Integration (SQL)
Joined transactional tables to calculate the base metrics:
 **Recency:** Days since the last purchase.
 **Frequency:** Total number of orders placed. 
 **Monetary:** Total revenue generated by the customer.

# 2. Statistical Deep Dive (Python)
 **EDA:** Analyzed distributions and identified outliers using box plots.
 **Segmentation:** Applied K-Means Clustering to group customers into 4 segments: VIP, Loyalists, At-Risk, and Lost.
 **Statistical Testing:** Conducted ANOVA to ensure segments were statistically distinct.

# 3. Visualization (Power BI)
Developed a dynamic dashboard to track:
- Segment distribution and revenue contribution.
- Geographical heatmaps of high-value customers.
- Trend analysis of purchase frequency over time.

# Key Insights & Recommendations
 **VIP Segment (15%):** Contributes to 45% of total revenue. Strategy: Early access to new products.
 **At-Risk Segment (25%):** Customers haven't purchased in 6+ months. Strategy: Re-engagement email coupons.
 **New Customers:** High potential but low frequency. Strategy: Welcome loyalty program.
